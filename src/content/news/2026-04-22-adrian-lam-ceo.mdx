**TORONTO, April 22, 2026** — CorVista Health today announced the appointment of Adrian Lam as President and Chief Executive Officer, effective immediately. Mr. Lam brings more than a decade of experience scaling medical technology companies across North America and Asia-Pacific.

"We are at an inflection point," said Tim Attebery, Chair of the CorVista Health Board of Directors. "With two FDA 510(k) clearances in hand and a third submission filed, the company is ready to accelerate commercial deployment. Adrian's track record of building clinical and commercial operations at scale makes him the right leader for this chapter."

Mr. Lam joins CorVista from his prior role leading commercial strategy for a global diagnostics company where he oversaw expansion into more than 40 markets. He will be based at the company's Toronto headquarters.

"I have followed CorVista's scientific work for years," said Mr. Lam. "The evidence base is extraordinary, and the clinical need is real. My focus will be getting the CorVista System into the hands of every front-line clinician who needs it."

## About CorVista Health

CorVista Health develops AI-powered cardiovascular diagnostic systems for the front line of care. The CorVista System is the only FDA-cleared device that assesses for coronary artery disease, pulmonary hypertension, and pulmonary capillary wedge pressure from a single resting, non-invasive capture. The company is headquartered in Toronto, Ontario.
